Who I am

About Me

I am a Computer Systems Engineering graduate from The Islamia University of Bahawalpur with interests in both embedded systems and intelligent software development. My focus is on building practical, real-world solutions that integrate hardware and software.

My final year project, Smart Stock Predictor, is a Flutter-based mobile application powered by a FastAPI backend, integrating machine learning models including Random Forest and FinBERT sentiment analysis, along with a Gemini-powered chatbot.

I have developed multiple embedded systems projects such as traffic control, greenhouse monitoring, and water tank automation using Arduino. Additionally, I completed a Flutter internship at PITB, gaining hands-on experience in mobile application development.

My academic background includes Neural Networks and Fuzzy Logic, and I maintain a strong interest in bridging embedded systems with modern cloud-based applications.
